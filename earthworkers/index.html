Meet Our Earthworkers

Earthworkers are the volunteers that make up For Every Star, A Tree.

Our volunteers normally contribute one (or more!) of the following values:

Play: A joyful, rebellious spirit

Work: A mindful, action-based movement

Care: A compassionate, intentional attention

Below, you'll find our growing network of Earthworkers who have quite literally helped us heal the Earth.

Our Team

As a 100% volunteer-led movement, we share leadership and stewardship decisions.

Everyone and anyone that is interested in cultivating a healthier planet is welcome to join us as an Earthworker. You can learn how to become an Earthworker here.

Our Mission

We are dedicated to cultivating desertified land.

We help regenerate Earth by inviting Earthworkers to plant trees, sow seeds, and build healthy soil.

We call this Earthcare. It's the act of everyday people (like you!) coming together to help care for the Earth.

Our primary site is in the heart of the western Mojave Desert. If you're in the Los Angeles county region and want to help, you can sign up to volunteer.

Ready to help?
