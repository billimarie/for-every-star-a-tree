Meet Our Earthworkers

For Every Star, A Tree is our poetic love letter to the earth.

Our global community of Earthworkers are dedicated to sharing our mission: to regenerate the earth, one plant at a time.

As nonprofit ecofarm, our mission is to steward desertified land into a thriving oasis.

We call this Earthcare: the act of everyday people, like our Earthworkers, helping us cultivating healthy soil for people, pollinators, and the planet.
