Our 2024 Impact Report

Last year, we raised $4,682. This helped us grow from 200sqft to a quarter-acre.

We hosted over 27 overnight stays at the land, earning $3,356.

More than 20 people donated, contributing $1,326.

We partnered with 8 new organizations.

More than 36 volunteers came out to help sow seeds, water plants, and move mulch.

Our Daring Greatly goal was to plant 1,000sqft.

We ended up planting more than 10,800sqft.

We added 5 individuals to our combined Board Member and Advisory Committees.

We hosted an Ecofest with Cosmic Labyrinth, who built our Sacred Toilet Temple outhouse.

A local storyteller & author, Cara Lopez Lee, raised $555 through her Candlelight Bridge sale donations.

This year, our Daring Greatly goal is to increase the Butterfly Garden's canopy by 50%.

Join our global community of backers helping us bring our eco-restoration work to life.
