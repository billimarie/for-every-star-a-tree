A Note About The Land

My name is Billimarie. I founded For Every Star, A Tree not as a project, but as a practice. I wanted to build the habit of caring for the Earth with my daughter, who was only 1 years old when we first came across the land.

forEST's vision is now something we can all share, carry forward, and strive toward as we work together with our hands, hearts, and attention.

My role is to continue as one of many stewards for this vision. For Every Star, A Tree belongs to the people and the land that it serves...and more importantly, to the generations who will inherit what we care for today.

The Starry Night Skoolie

We renovated an abandoned school bus in the middle of the desert.

Affectionately known as The Starry Night Skoolie, it functions as our cozy clubhouse and humble place of refuge.

It's also a 5-Stary stay on Airbnb.

We shut it down due to extensive damage by a former guest. You can help bring the Skoolie back to life by donating directly to our renovation fund.
