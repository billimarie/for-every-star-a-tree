For Every Star, A Tree is growing a Tiny Forest in the heart of the Desert.

This is our climate love letter to the Earth.

We call ourselves Earthworkers.

We are a nonprofit, grassroots movement inviting everyone to reconnect with land through stewardship, restoration, & care.

Since 2023, our vision has remained the same: let's work together to grow a self-sustaining oasis...one square foot at a time.

A Note About The Land

My name is Billimarie. I founded For Every Star, A Tree not as a project, but as a practice for my daughter & I.

forEST's vision, to grow a sustainable oasis in the desert, is now something everyone can share.

My role is to continue as one of many stewards in service to healing this Earth. For Every Star, A Tree belongs to the people & the land that help it grow...

...& more importantly, to the generations we will never meet, who will inherit what we care for today.

The Starry Night Skoolie

We renovated an abandoned school bus in the middle of the desert.

Affectionately known as The Starry Night Skoolie, it functions as our cozy clubhouse & humble place of refuge.

It's also a 5-Star stay on Airbnb.

We shut it down due to extensive damage by a former guest. You can help bring the Skoolie back to life by donating directly to our renovation fund.
