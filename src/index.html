our mission

Climate joy

Be part of the desert forest dream

Since 2021, we have planted over 100 trees, wildflowers, grasses, and shrubs throughout 10 acres of desert land. We strive to heal abandonded land by cultivating gardens and forests. We do this by accepting donations from generous people like you.

We believe in inviting people from around the globe to contribute to afforestation on a personal level. That's why we're inviting you to plant your own tree from anywhere in the world.

Together, we can grow this special land into a green oasis.

We make tree planting personal

In October 2023, we will start planting a Miyawaki Forest. We will be starting with 100ft2 and growing from there.

First, select your tree or gift one to somebody special. Next, you'll get an invite with the date and time of your planting. Everyone's trees will be planted on the same day, so that we can all participate together.

If you want regular inner circle updates about your tree, you can subscribe for monthly text messages, videos, pictures, 24/7 livestreaming, and more. This can also be gifted to a friend.
